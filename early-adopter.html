Frequently Asked Questions

What's the difference between Founding Member and Early Adopter?

The Founding Member tier offers lifetime access to PreBoot Pro with all future updates included, plus exclusive benefits like a consultation call and special recognition. The Early Adopter tier provides a one-year subscription at a discounted rate with priority support and early access to features.

When will PreBoot Pro be officially released?

We plan to officially release PreBoot Pro in Q3 2025. Early adopters get immediate access to current features and all updates during development.

What happens after I purchase?

After your purchase, you'll receive an email with instructions to set up your PreBoot Pro account. This includes access to the private Maven/npm repositories, documentation, and an invitation to the private Discord channel. Founding Members will also receive a schedule link to book their consultation call.

Can I upgrade from Early Adopter to Founding Member?

Yes, you can upgrade from Early Adopter to Founding Member during the early adopter period by paying the difference. Contact our support team after your purchase if you wish to upgrade.

What if I need help implementing PreBoot in my project?

Both Early Adopter tiers include priority support through our Discord channel and email. Founding Members also receive a 1-hour consultation call which can be used for implementation guidance. For additional support needs, we offer consulting services at an hourly rate for complex implementation projects.
