Frequently Asked Questions

What's included in the Professional Edition?

PreBoot Pro includes advanced multi-tenancy strategies, enhanced security features, white-labeling capabilities, premium UI components, priority support, and exclusive access to premium content and courses at a significant discount.

What is the Early Adopter program?

The Early Adopter program gives you access to PreBoot Pro at a significant discount. Early adopters also get to directly influence the roadmap, receive personalized support, and get recognized as founding community members.

Can I upgrade from Community to Professional?

Yes, you can easily upgrade from Community to Professional at any time. Your existing projects will continue to work, and you'll gain access to additional features and capabilities.

Do you offer educational discounts?

Yes, we offer discounts for educational institutions, non-profits, and open source projects. Contact us for more information.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept credit cards, PayPal, and bank transfers for annual enterprise contracts.

What's the refund policy?

We offer a 30-day money-back guarantee for Professional Edition. If you're not satisfied, contact us within 30 days for a full refund.
